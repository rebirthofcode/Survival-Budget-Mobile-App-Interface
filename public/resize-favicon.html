Favicon Resizer

Resize your favicon.png to all required sizes for the web app

📋 Instructions

  1. Click "Choose Image File" below and select your favicon.png
  2. Wait for the previews to appear (should be instant)
  3. Click "Download" under each size to save that version
  4. Save all files to the public/ folder (overwrite if they exist)
  5. Your favicons will be ready to use!
